About Smart Cart

Revolutionizing everyday shopping through innovation, autonomy, and accessibility.

Our Story

Smart Cart was born from a real need to make shopping easier and more accessible for everyone. Traditional shopping carts are heavy, awkward, and often broken — a challenge for seniors, parents, or anyone carrying bags or pushing a stroller.

Our autonomous cart follows you safely and smoothly without physical effort. Using advanced sensors, a vision camera, and AI algorithms, Smart Cart recognizes its user, navigates through aisles, and moves at your pace — no pushing required.

The control system combines microcontrollers and FPGA boards that manage motors and steering mechanisms, ensuring precise movement and real-time decision-making. The cart’s modular design allows future features like product scanning, shelf navigation, and automatic checkout.

Our vision is to create a smart, friendly, and accessible shopping experience by bringing cutting-edge technology into daily life. Smart Cart — the future of effortless shopping.
